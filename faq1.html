Frequently Asked Questions

What do you do if you want to securely park your vehicle at the parking lot?

You need to reveal you HZ pass at the barriers so you can get through and into the parking lot. Due to the expansion of the building, the parking spaces are limited and reserved only for those who truly need it.

How can one book a room for a group study or meeting?

First, either go to the HZ portal or HZ University top Desk. From here on out you need to follow the following path: Reserve meeting room or Project Group room > Reserve a room

Now all you have to do is locate the proper location and drag the hours on the desired row.

How to print a document from your laptop or printer at Hz

First to go to the online HZ print webpage. log into your account and upload all necessary files you want to print. Go to the printer, scan your card in it, choose the printing option and hit print.
