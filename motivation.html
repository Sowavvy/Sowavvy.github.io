What is motivation

Motivation is what prompts a person to perform certain behavior, in humans motivation is mainly about the initiation, direction, commitment and persistence of the behavior. Motivation arises from the combination of innate and cultural characteristics of the individual. Furthermore, rewards have a lot of influence on the motivation to do something or to perform something. Research has shown that positive results and rewards have a lot of influence on motivation and that there appears to be more motivation than when there are negative results and rewards.
